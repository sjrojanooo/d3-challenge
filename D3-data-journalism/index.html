Analysis Between Household Income (Median) and Smokes (%):
Household Incomes that earning a low yearly wage result in homes having higher number of smokers. This could be an impact from financial instability that results in anxiety, stress, and depression. The homes that earn a higher yearly salary contain the lowest amount of smokers.

Analysis Between Poverty(%) and Obesity(%): The most poverish parts of the country hold the highest score of obesity. This could be a result of lower access and purchasing power to good quality food. One should also consider the pyschological affect that financial instability causes. Stress, and unhealthy eating habits are a culpurate to weight gain and low physical activity.
